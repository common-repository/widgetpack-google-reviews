WidgetPack Google Reviews can display more than 5 Google Reviews on your website to increase user confidence and search engine optimization.

== Description ==

> > To get more unique features we have <a href="https://richplugins.com/google-reviews-pro-wordpress-plugin">Pro and Business version</a> plugin

WidgetPack Google Reviews display Google Business Rating and Reviews on your websites. Our widget can get more than 5 Google Reviews because every time when you get Google Reviews, they are saved and collected in your account and thus can be accumulated 5 or more reviews.

[youtube https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YccWFCkz6H4]

[Online demo](http://demo.richplugins.com/)

= Plugin Features =

* It's free!
* Display up to 5 Google business reviews per location
* Shows real business reviews from Google+ users
* Work without Google Places API key
* Change size and color of review stars
* Easy search of place and instantly show reviews
* Themes: review list and Google badge box (fixed, inner)
* Trim long reviews and add "read more" link
* Support dark websites
* Zero load time regardless of your website
* Shows Google reviews even if Google is unavailable
* Free support on WP.org

== Installation ==

1. Unpack archive to this archive to the 'wp-content/plugins/' directory inside of WordPress
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
